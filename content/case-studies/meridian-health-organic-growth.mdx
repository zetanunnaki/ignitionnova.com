## Executive Summary

Meridian Health, a regional healthcare provider, was virtually invisible in search results for their most critical service keywords. Our comprehensive SEO strategy moved them from page 5 to position 1 for 12 target keywords within 90 days, doubling their organic traffic and delivering a 4.2x return on investment.

## The Challenge

Healthcare SEO is uniquely challenging due to Google's E-E-A-T requirements and YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) classification. Meridian faced:

- Rankings on page 5+ for core service keywords like "orthopedic surgeon [city]"
- A website with 4,200 pages but only 800 indexed
- Thin content that failed to demonstrate medical expertise
- Zero backlinks from authoritative medical publications
- 78% bounce rate on service pages

## Our Strategy

### Technical Foundation (Weeks 1-3)

- Pruned 2,100 low-quality pages that were diluting crawl budget
- Fixed 340 broken internal links and redirect chains
- Implemented medical-specific schema (MedicalOrganization, Physician, MedicalProcedure)
- Achieved sub-2-second load times across all service pages

### Content Authority (Weeks 3-8)

- Created 24 comprehensive condition and procedure guides authored by Meridian physicians
- Each guide included author credentials, medical citations, and last-reviewed dates
- Built a symptom-checker interactive tool that generated natural backlinks
- Established author pages with verified medical credentials

### Authority Building (Weeks 4-12)

- Secured placements in 8 medical publications and health directories
- Built relationships with 12 local health organizations for natural link building
- Launched a community health education program that generated press coverage

## Results After 90 Days

The combination of technical excellence, authoritative content, and strategic link building produced dramatic results across every metric Meridian tracked.

Patient inquiry forms increased by 156%, and the cost per patient acquisition dropped from $340 to $82 — a transformation that directly impacted their bottom line.
