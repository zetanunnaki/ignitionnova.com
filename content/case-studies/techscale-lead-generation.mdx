## Executive Summary

TechScale Inc., a B2B SaaS platform serving mid-market companies, came to Ignition Nova with a common but critical problem: their website was generating traffic but failing to convert visitors into marketing-qualified leads. Within 6 months of our engagement, we delivered a 340% increase in MQLs while reducing cost per acquisition by 45%.

## The Challenge

TechScale's existing website suffered from several compounding issues:

- **Slow page loads** (6.2s average LCP) causing 68% bounce rate on mobile
- **Poor technical SEO** resulting in only 23% of pages indexed by Google
- **Zero conversion optimization** — the site had one generic "Contact Us" form
- **Broken analytics** making it impossible to attribute leads to channels

## Our Approach

### Phase 1: Technical Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

We performed a comprehensive technical audit that revealed 847 issues across crawlability, indexation, and performance. Our engineering team:

- Rebuilt the frontend on Next.js, achieving a 98 Lighthouse score
- Implemented proper schema markup across all 200+ pages
- Fixed canonical issues causing duplicate content penalties
- Set up server-side tracking with GA4 and proper attribution

### Phase 2: Conversion Architecture (Weeks 5-8)

We redesigned the conversion funnel based on user behavior data:

- Created role-specific landing pages for each ICP segment
- Implemented progressive profiling forms that adapt to visitor stage
- Added social proof elements (metrics, logos, testimonials) at key decision points
- Built an ROI calculator as an interactive lead magnet

### Phase 3: Content & CRO Experimentation (Weeks 9-24)

We launched a systematic testing program:

- Ran 23 A/B tests across landing pages, CTAs, and form designs
- Published 12 high-authority content pieces targeting bottom-funnel keywords
- Optimized the blog-to-demo conversion path with smart CTAs

## The Results

The compounding effect of technical excellence, conversion optimization, and content strategy delivered transformative results:

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Monthly MQLs | 47 | 207 | +340% |
| Cost per MQL | $312 | $171 | -45% |
| Organic Traffic | 12,400 | 31,000 | +150% |
| Lighthouse Score | 34 | 98 | +188% |
| Pages Indexed | 23% | 97% | +322% |

## Key Takeaway

Technical SEO and CRO aren't separate disciplines — they're multipliers. Fixing TechScale's technical foundation didn't just improve rankings; it improved every downstream metric by providing a faster, more trustworthy experience.
