## Executive Summary

NexGen Financial became the first financial services firm to implement a comprehensive Generative Engine Optimization strategy. Within 4 months, their brand was cited in 85% of relevant AI-generated search results across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity — establishing a competitive moat that competitors are now scrambling to replicate.

## The Opportunity

NexGen's CMO recognized a shift: their target demographic (affluent millennials planning for retirement) was increasingly using AI chatbots instead of Google for financial planning research. He came to us with a simple question: "How do we become the brand that AI recommends?"

## Our Approach

### Phase 1: AI Visibility Audit

We developed a proprietary testing methodology, querying 500+ financial planning questions across 6 AI platforms and tracking which brands were cited. The baseline was sobering: NexGen appeared in 0% of AI-generated answers. Their competitors? Also 0%. The field was wide open.

### Phase 2: Entity Architecture

We rebuilt NexGen's digital identity from the ground up:

- Created comprehensive entity profiles across Wikidata, Crunchbase, and industry databases
- Structured their advisory team's credentials with Person schema and verifiable expertise signals
- Built entity relationships connecting NexGen to key financial planning concepts

### Phase 3: Citable Content Engine

We launched a content program specifically designed for AI citation:

- Published 36 original research reports with proprietary data
- Created definitive guides on 15 core financial planning topics
- Established NexGen advisors as quoted experts in 20+ financial publications
- Built a financial planning calculator cited by multiple AI platforms

### Phase 4: Continuous Monitoring

We deployed AI citation monitoring across all major platforms, tracking:

- Citation frequency and accuracy
- Competitor citation rates
- Query coverage breadth
- Sentiment and context of citations

## The Results

NexGen now dominates AI search for financial planning queries in their market. The quality of inbound leads improved dramatically — prospects arriving through AI recommendations were 3.8x more likely to convert than those from traditional search, because AI citations carry implicit endorsement.

The first-mover advantage in GEO is real and compounding. As NexGen's citation rate grows, AI models increasingly treat them as the authoritative source, making it harder for competitors to displace them.
